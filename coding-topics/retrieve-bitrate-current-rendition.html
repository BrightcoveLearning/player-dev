What playlist is used?

In the code a playlist is referenced:

myPlayer.tech(true).vhs.playlists.media().attributes.BANDWIDTH

This is NOT referring to a playlist of videos. Rather it is referencing an HLS manifest of renditions, which is also referred to as a playlist. For instance, in this video there are both HLS and MP4 renditions (the HLS renditions, or HLS playlist, are hightlighted):
